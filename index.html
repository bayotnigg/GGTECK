GENERAL SCIENCE

- General Science or science is one of the most important channels of knowledge and plays a vital role in the preparation of competitive examinations. Studying subjects like Physics, Chemistry & Biology is important for the general understanding of things around us.

In general, science consists of a pursuit of knowledge that covers general truths or the operations of fundamental laws.

BIOLOGY
Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms , focusing on their structure, function, growth, evolution, and interactions with their environment. It encompasses various branches, including genetics, ecology, microbiology, physiology, and evolution. Learn more about BIOLOGY

CHEMISTRY
Chemistry is the study of matter, which is everything around us. It focuses on understanding what substances are made of, how they interact with each other, and how they change. Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space, like the air you breathe, the food you eat, or the water you drink.

At the heart of chemistry is the idea that everything around us, no matter how complex, can be broken down into basic chemical components that follow certain rules and patterns. It’s a science that touches almost every part of life! Learn more about CHEMISTRY

PHYSICS
Physics is the study of the forces and energy that shape the universe. It’s all about understanding how things move, interact, and change. Physics explains everything from why an apple falls from a tree to how the stars in the sky shine.

At its core, physics focuses on fundamental concepts like motion, energy, force, and matter. It helps us understand how objects move and why things behave the way they do. For example, it explains why cars speed up or slow down, why the Earth orbits the Sun, and how electricity powers our devices. Learn more about PHYSICS
